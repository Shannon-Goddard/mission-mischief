PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: December 2024
Key Point: Mission Mischief stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Mission Mischief, you may provide:

  • Username and profile information
  • Mission completion data
  • Photos taken during gameplay (stored locally only)
  • Social media content you create related to the game

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent)
  • Technical data for game functionality

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Improve game features and functionality
  • Communicate with you about the game
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. DATA STORAGE

Local Storage Only: Mission Mischief uses your device's local storage (localStorage) to save your game progress, settings, and data. This information never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

3.1 Photos and Media

All photos taken within the game (including mugshots) are:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never uploaded to our servers
  • Under your complete control
  • Deletable by you at any time

4. INFORMATION SHARING

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transfer

5. SOCIAL MEDIA

Mission Mischief encourages social media sharing. When you post about the game:

  • You control what information you share
  • Your posts are subject to the social media platform's privacy policy
  • We may view and reference public posts for promotional purposes

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Mission Mischief uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Local storage for game data
  • Session storage for temporary data
  • No third-party tracking cookies
  • No analytics or advertising trackers

7. YOUR RIGHTS

7.1 General Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information
  • Restrict processing of your information
  • Data portability

7.2 California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

7.3 EU Residents (GDPR)

EU residents have rights including:

  • Right of access and portability
  • Right to rectification and erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

8. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local storage encryption where available
  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited data collection minimizes risk

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Mission Mischief is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. DATA RETENTION

Since data is stored locally on your device:

  • You control how long data is retained
  • You can delete game data at any time
  • Uninstalling the game removes all local data
  • We retain minimal server logs for 30 days maximum

11. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Since Mission Mischief stores data locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Any minimal server communication occurs within the United States.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on our website
  • Updating the effective date
  • Providing notice within the game if significant

13. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Mission Mischief may integrate with:

  • Social media platforms (for sharing)
  • Payment processors (for purchases)
  • These services have their own privacy policies
